MTN PUR was founded in Omaha in 2019 with one conviction: the places we live and work should be optimized for health — not just free of what makes us sick.

Our Story

Why "Mountain Pure"?

Have you ever noticed how the air feels different in the mountains? It's crisp. It's clean. It's the kind of air that makes you feel like you can breathe deeper, think clearer, and simply feel better. There's a reason for that — and it's not magic. It's science.

Mountain air is clean largely because it's dry. And where there's no moisture, mold can't thrive. That's the standard we're chasing for every home and workspace we touch.

"Not just air that won't make you sick — air that helps you thrive."

MTN PUR is short for Mountain Pure. mtnpur.com was available, it's simple, and it says exactly what we do.

Founded

August 2019 — Pure Maintenance Nebraska opened its doors in Omaha, Nebraska. Within months, COVID-19 arrived and demand for professional sanitization services surged. We were ready — rooted in the same disinfection science that protects medical facilities.

Our Background

Our founder Jeff spent years working on dirt bikes, cars, and airplanes — taking things that already worked and making them perform better. MTN PUR applies that same philosophy to the places you live and work.

Today

Now operating as MTN PUR, we serve homeowners and businesses across Omaha, Papillion, Lincoln, and the surrounding area — with plans to expand to Des Moines and beyond.

How We Think

A different approach to mold — and to business

Most mold companies treat what they can see. MTN PUR treats the entire environment. Most companies lead with fear. We lead with education. Here's what that looks like in practice.

01
Educate first

We don't believe in using fear to drive decisions. We explain what we find, what it means, and what the options are — in plain language. An informed customer makes a better decision for their home and family.

02
Treat the whole home

Mold isn't a visible problem — it's a microscopic one. There are far more mold sources than most people realize in a typical home. Our process addresses every cubic foot of air and every square inch of surface, not just what's in front of us.

03
Build lasting results

Treatment is the beginning, not the end. We educate customers on humidity management, connect them with vetted partners for moisture control, and stay in touch with practical tips to protect their investment long term.

What We Do

Two services. One complete process.

Every MTN PUR treatment begins with a thorough inspection and ends with a whole-home process that addresses what traditional remediation leaves behind.

Step One

MTN Scrub

Before we treat, we prepare. Our HEPA-powered scrubbing process removes bio-nesting sites — the flat surfaces where mold spores land and colonize. Cabinet tops, window sills, door frames, and other surfaces are thoroughly cleaned while commercial air scrubbers run simultaneously to pull airborne particles from the environment.

Step Two

MTN Sanitization

Once the environment is prepped, MTN Sanitization — using the proprietary Pure Maintenance dry vapor process — reaches every corner, crevice, and surface that traditional remediation misses. Every cubic foot of air, every square inch of surface area in your home. It doesn't just treat the problem in front of you. It treats the whole home.
